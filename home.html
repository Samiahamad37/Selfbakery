Our Story

Baked with
Passion & Tradition

Zelfbaker was born from a simple love of baking bread. What started as a hobby in our home kitchen grew into a passion for artisan baking with the finest organic ingredients.

Every product is made by hand — from our crispy sourdough to our buttery croissants — completely free from artificial additives.
