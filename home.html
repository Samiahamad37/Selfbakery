Our Story

Born in Dar es Salaam,
Baked with Ujuzi

Self Bakery started as a small home kitchen operation in Kinondoni, driven by a love of baking and a belief that Dar es Salaam deserved truly great bread. Three years on, we bake hundreds of loaves, mandazi, and cakes every week.

Every item is made by hand, using fresh local ingredients from Tanzanian suppliers — no preservatives, no shortcuts, just honest baking delivered to your door.

✓ 100% fresh local ingredients
✓ No artificial additives or preservatives
✓ Baked fresh every morning from 5am
✓ Delivery across Dar es Salaam
✓ Custom orders for weddings & events
