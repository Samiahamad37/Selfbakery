A Webshop Built for

M-Pesa Payment Integration

Tanzanian Shilling (TSh) Pricing

Business Impact

How This Website Grows
Self Bakery

Every feature was chosen with one outcome in mind: turning Self Bakery's talent into consistent, growing income in Dar es Salaam.

📍

Reaches Every Dar es Salaam Neighbourhood

A bakery in Kinondoni doesn't have to rely on foot traffic from Kinondoni alone. Anyone in DSM can browse the menu at midnight, place an order, pay via M-Pesa, and get fresh bread in the morning. The whole city becomes your market.

💬

Ends the WhatsApp Order Chaos

Managing orders across Instagram DMs, WhatsApp messages, and phone calls is exhausting and error-prone. A webshop centralises every order with clear item names, quantities, and addresses — so you bake exactly what you need, nothing more.

📱

M-Pesa Makes Payment Instant and Secure

No more chasing cash payments, dealing with bounced mobile transfers, or awkward "I'll pay on delivery" situations. M-Pesa payment details are shown directly in the cart — customers pay before you bake, so cash flow improves immediately.

📈

The TSh 25,000 Threshold Increases Every Order

When a customer is at TSh 18,000, the cart tells them they're TSh 7,000 away from free delivery. Almost every time, they add a mandazi pack or a banana cake slice. This single mechanic can increase your average order value by 20–30%.

🏆

Premium Design Justifies Premium Pricing

A professional, beautiful website signals quality. Customers who see a well-designed menu with real photos, proper descriptions, and glowing reviews are far less likely to ask "can you do it cheaper?" The brand commands better prices from day one.

🎂

Unlocks Corporate & Event Orders

Wedding cakes, office catering, graduation parties — these are high-value orders that currently go to whoever has the most visible online presence. A professional website puts Self Bakery in the running for contracts worth TSh 500,000+ per order.
